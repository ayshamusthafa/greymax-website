About Us

Grey Max General Contracting & Maintenance

based in Abu Dhabi, stands out as a highly regarded firm in commercial, institutional, and industrial construction. With a focus on medium-sized projects, we consistently deliver on time, within budget, and with top-notch quality. Our reputation for excellence stems from our core values, including enthusiasm for our work, customer sensitivity, and a commitment to responsibility and craftsmanship. Located in Abu Dhabi, UAE, our success lies in prioritizing customer satisfaction, managing projects closely, and providing tailored construction management services. Our hands-on project management approach allows us to effectively meet deadlines and maintain quality standards. With over 250 skilled employees, we are dedicated to exceeding customer expectations. Our commitment to service, integrity, and quality defines us, and we stake our name and reputation on it. Our portfolio includes villas, warehouses, offices, and industrial buildings, all completed to client satisfaction within agreed timelines. Commitment is our strongest trait, evident in our dedication to clients, team members, and industry standards. We continue to enhance our preconstruction and construction management services, expecting the upcoming year to be one of our best.
